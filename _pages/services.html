Our Services

At LumoLetters, we specialize in creating personalized, heartfelt letters that foster connection and bring joy. Whether it's for a resident in a nursing home, a family member, or anyone looking to share meaningful stories, our service is designed to create a sense of community through the written word.

Personalized Letters Tailored to You

Each letter is carefully crafted to reflect the recipient's personality and interests. We offer a wide range of themes and tones, ensuring every message feels personal and genuine. From friendly check-ins to thoughtful musings, every letter is a unique reflection of your connection.

Easy to Use for Families and Caretakers

Our service is built with simplicity in mind, so it's accessible for everyone, including those who may not be tech-savvy. Whether you're a family member, a caretaker, or a friend, sending a letter to brighten someone's day has never been easier.

Specializing in Nursing Homes

While LumoLetters is for anyone who wants to create meaningful connections, we specialize in supporting residents in nursing homes. Our platform is designed to help foster communication, reduce isolation, and improve the quality of life for seniors. By providing them with regular, personalized letters, we offer a way to stay engaged and connected, even when physical visits are limited.

Letter Series & Story Building

LumoLetters is more than just sending a letter—it’s about creating an ongoing narrative. Each letter can be part of a larger story that develops over time. With our letter series, the recipient can look forward to the continuation of an exciting or heartwarming story, exchanged across multiple letters.

  • Personalized Storylines: Create a unique storyline that is tailored to your loved one’s preferences. From daily musings to grand adventures, we build a series of letters that form a larger narrative.
  • Culmination in a Keepsake: At the end of the series, the letters can be compiled into a special keepsake book, preserving the messages and memories shared.

A Story Told Over Time

One of the unique aspects of LumoLetters is the ability to build a story over time. Each letter contributes to a growing narrative, creating a continuous dialogue between you and the recipient. This ongoing communication can even be compiled into a book or special keepsake for both the sender and receiver.

Flexibility for Any Need

Whether you’re sending a one-time letter for a special occasion or creating a long-term correspondence, our service is flexible to your needs. You can choose the frequency of the letters, the tone, and the style, ensuring it’s just right for the recipient.

Custom Themes and Occasions

Whether it’s for a birthday, anniversary, or a special holiday, LumoLetters can create custom themed letters for any occasion. This service provides the perfect way to celebrate milestones, offer congratulations, or simply brighten someone's day with a thoughtful gesture.

  • Occasion-Based Letters: Birthdays, holidays, and more—our letters are designed with the special occasion in mind.
  • Seasonal Themes: We offer letters that reflect the beauty of each season, from spring blossoms to winter coziness, tailored to the recipient's tastes.

Caregiver and Family Support

Our services extend beyond the individual receiving the letters to provide additional support for caregivers and families. LumoLetters aims to offer a valuable resource to those supporting elderly loved ones, helping to reduce feelings of isolation, stress, and uncertainty.

  • Caregiver Resources: We offer suggestions for fostering meaningful connections through letters, as well as ways for families to get involved in the process.
  • Family Involvement: Caregivers can play an active role in the letter exchange, helping to reinforce bonds between family members and their loved ones.
  • Remote Communication: For families that cannot be there in person, LumoLetters bridges the gap by offering a way for them to send messages and maintain regular communication with their elderly family members.

Subscription Plans

To make it easier for families and caregivers to manage regular communication, LumoLetters offers subscription plans that fit a variety of needs and budgets. Whether you want to send a letter every week or month, we have a plan that works for you.

  • Flexible Frequency: Choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly letters.
  • Customizable Letter Quantities: Need more letters in a month? We can accommodate special requests.
  • Affordable Packages: We offer different pricing tiers to ensure that you can access our services at a price point that fits your needs.
