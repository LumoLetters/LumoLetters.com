Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LumoLetters ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control them.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to website owners.

We also use similar technologies such as local storage and session storage (collectively referred to as "cookies" in this policy).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use only essential cookies that are necessary for our website to function properly. These cookies enable core functionality such as:

Maintaining your login session when you access your account

Remembering your authentication status

Protecting against security threats and fraudulent activity

Enabling basic site navigation and access to secure areas

We do not use cookies for:

Tracking your browsing activity across other websites

Analytics or performance monitoring

Marketing or advertising purposes

Building user profiles or behavioral targeting

3. Cookies We Use

The table below lists the essential cookies used on our website:

Cookie Name Purpose Provider Duration
auth0 Manages user authentication and login sessions Auth0 (Third-party) Session
auth0_compat Ensures compatibility across different browsers for authentication Auth0 (Third-party) Session
_auth0_session Stores encrypted user session information Auth0 (Third-party) 7 days
session_id Maintains your browsing session on our website LumoLetters Session
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks LumoLetters Session

Note: "Session" cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some of the cookies listed above are set by Auth0, our authentication provider. These cookies are essential for secure login functionality. Auth0's use of cookies is governed by their own privacy policy, which you can review at https://auth0.com/privacy.

We do not allow any other third-party cookies on our website.

5. Managing Cookies

Because we only use essential cookies that are necessary for our website to function, we do not provide a cookie consent banner. These cookies cannot be disabled without preventing you from using key features of our service, such as logging into your account.

However, you can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that blocking or deleting essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and accessing your LumoLetters account. For information on how to manage cookies in your browser, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
