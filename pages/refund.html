Refund Policy

At LumoLetters, we strive to deliver high-quality personalized letters and subscriptions. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued.

1. Subscription Refunds

Subscription payments are non-refundable once a letter has been processed or delivered. Cancellations will prevent future letters from being sent but will not retroactively refund previously delivered letters.

2. Service Issues

If you experience a technical issue or error that prevents delivery, please contact info@LumoLetters.com within 7 days. We will review your request and may issue a partial or full refund at our discretion.

3. Unauthorized Charges

Any unauthorized charges should be reported immediately. LumoLetters will investigate and, if verified, provide a refund for unauthorized billing.

4. Contact Information

For refund inquiries, please reach out to info@LumoLetters.com.
