Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LumoLetters

Getting Started

Getting started is simple:

  1. Create Your Profile: Tell us about your interests through our personalized intake form.
  2. Choose Your Plan: Select how often you'd like to receive letters (1, 2, or 4 times per month).
  3. Receive Your Letters: We craft and mail personalized letters directly to your mailbox, complete with return envelopes.
  4. Write Back (Optional): Continue the conversation at your own pace, or simply enjoy receiving letters.

Delivery & Logistics

After completing your profile and selecting your topics, your first letter is typically mailed within 3-5 business days. Delivery time depends on your location, but most letters arrive within 3-7 days after mailing. You'll receive tracking notifications in your dashboard.

Currently, LumoLetters is available to addresses within the United States. We're working on expanding to international shipping in the future. Contact us to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

You can update your mailing address anytime from your account settings. Please update your address at least 7 days before your next scheduled letter to ensure uninterrupted delivery.
