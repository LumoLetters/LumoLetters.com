Your Questions Answered

Common Questions

We believe in transparency. Here are answers to some questions you might have.

Who actually writes the letters?

We use a thoughtful two-step process: First, artificial intelligence generates a draft letter personalized to your specific interests. Then, our human editorial team reviews, edits, and curates every letter to ensure it meets our standards for quality, coherence, and warmth. Think of it as AI-assisted, human-perfected correspondence.

Is my information private and secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our priority. We use your information solely to create your letters and never sell or share it for marketing. All data is encrypted, and we work with trusted, secure partners. You can read the full details in our comprehensive Privacy Policy.

What if a topic doesn't interest me?

You're in control. You can update your interests at any time through your account dashboard. Future letters will reflect your new preferences. We encourage you to explore a variety of topics, but the journey is yours to shape.

Can I really cancel anytime?

Yes, without any hassle. You can cancel your subscription directly from your account settings at any time. There are no long-term contracts or hidden fees.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship physical letters to addresses within the United States. We hope to expand internationally in the future.

What's your refund policy?

We do not offer refunds for partial subscription periods, as each letter is personalized and produced upon order. However, you can cancel at any time to stop future charges. Please review our Terms of Service for complete details.
